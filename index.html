My Life of Travel: The Beginning

Here’s the short story.

On December 25th, 1999, I left the USA for a three-month, post-graduation trip to Southeast Asia. The idea was to backpack around Thailand, Cambodia and Vietnam and then return home and start a career in the sports industry (I wanted to be a Sports Agent).

When I landed in Bangkok, I had $1500 USD to my name and that was it. So I booked a $1 per night dorm room bed inside of a crumbling old wooden guesthouse in the center of the city and let the adventure begin from there.

The funny thing is, that adventure is still going on today. In the end, I never went home after those three months.

And it’s now been over 20 years of nonstop travel.

Go figure, I never would have imagined this would be my life!
